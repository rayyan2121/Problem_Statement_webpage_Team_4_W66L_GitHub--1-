Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

To sign up, download the WeTrip app from the App Store or Google Play Store, and follow the registration steps by providing your personal information and verifying your identity.

To transfer money, log in to your WeTrip account, select the "Transfer Money" option, enter the recipient's details, the amount to transfer, and confirm the transaction.

WeTrip charges a 0.5% exchange fee rate for international transactions. For more details on our fee structure, please visit our Fees page.

Time for transfers varies and it ranges from a few seconds and up to 2 business days depending on the recipient's country and bank processing times.

You can cancel a transfer if it is still in a pending status. Once the transfer has been processed, it cannot be canceled. Please contact our customer support team for assistance.
